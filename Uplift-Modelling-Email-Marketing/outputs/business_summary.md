# Business Summary

## Objective

Identify customers who are most likely to respond because of the email campaign.

## Main Results

- Total customers: 64,000
- Customers recommended for targeting: 12,800
- Treated conversion rate: 0.0107
- Control conversion rate: 0.0057
- Absolute uplift: 0.0050
- Relative lift: 86.53%
- Treated model AUC: 0.5941
- Control model AUC: 0.6231
- Top 20% actual conversion rate: 0.0205
- Top 20% average predicted uplift: 0.0127

## Recommendation

Target the top 20% of customers ranked by predicted uplift instead of sending emails to every customer. This focuses the campaign on customers most likely to change behaviour because of the email.
